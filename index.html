Who We Are

Angels Of Yamama Non-Profit Organization is a humanitarian organization dedicated to supporting the underprivileged and vulnerable populations in Sierra Leone, with a strong focus on children, families, and underserved communities in Port Loko District.

Our mission is to reduce health disparities, fight preventable illnesses, and empower communities through compassionate care, education, and sustainable support systems.

Our Mission

To improve the quality of life for underprivileged communities in Sierra Leone by providing access to healthcare, nutrition, education, and preventive health awareness, with special attention to children and individuals facing health disparities.

Our Vision

A healthier Sierra Leone where no child or family is denied care due to poverty, and where preventable diseases are addressed through early treatment, education, and community engagement.

Sierra Leone: Where We Serve

Why We Do It

Many communities in Sierra Leone still face limited access to basic healthcare, leading to preventable illness, complications, and loss of life.

Angels Of Yamama exists to bridge that gap, ensuring care reaches those who need it most. We are committed to serving Port Loko District and surrounding communities with compassion and dedication.

What We Do

Healthcare Access & Community Clinic

We are working to establish and support a community health care center in Port Loko, providing affordable and accessible care to those in need.

  • Wound care and infection management
  • Malaria diagnosis and treatment
  • Diarrhea and dehydration management
  • Typhoid fever treatment
  • Seizure management and referral care
  • Hypertensive crisis screening
  • Diabetes screening and education

Child & Family Support

Supporting families and children facing extreme hardship with essential resources and care.

  • Feeding programs for vulnerable children
  • Basic medical support for children in need
  • Assistance for families facing extreme hardship

Health Education & Disease Prevention

We believe prevention saves lives. Our outreach programs focus on educating communities.

  • Community health education
  • Preventive care awareness
  • Hygiene and sanitation education
  • Chronic disease prevention

Outreach Platforms:

  • Social media health campaigns
  • Local radio programs
  • Community awareness visits
